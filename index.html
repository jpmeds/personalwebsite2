JP MEDS

Instructor, Graphic Artist, Memer, Student, and a Friend

Hey there! I’m John Phillip M. Medina—an instructor, graphic artist, and meme lover born on November 14, 1999, and I’m a Scorpio. I graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology, specializing in Web System Technology, from NEUST. Right now, I’m working on my Master of Science in Information Technology with a focus on System Development.


During the day, I teach part-time at NEUST Sumacab. I like to add a bit of fun to my classes by using memes to make things more interesting. I also enjoy graphic design and creating eye-catching visuals. When I’m not teaching or designing, I’m listening to pop music from artists like Ariana Grande, Charli XCX, Chapell Roan, and Sabrina Carpenter.


As an introvert and a chronic online presence, I also love watching films and writing reviews about them on my Letterboxd account. It’s a great way for me to unwind and share my thoughts on the movies I watch. If you’re into movies and enjoy a good review, let’s connect and talk about our latest finds!
